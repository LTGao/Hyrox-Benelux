The 30–44 Core

The 30–44 age band consistently dominates participation. Prime career age, high disposable income, strong fitness identity. This demographic responds to premium race-day experience, travel packages, and recovery and nutrition brands.

📈
Female Participation Growing Every Season

From S4 to S8, the female share has grown consistently. Women-first campaign assets and category-specific sponsorship in apparel and wellness are underserved in fitness race marketing and represent high-ROI territory.

Nationality

Where they come from

81% of Benelux HYROX athletes are Dutch, Belgian or Luxembourgish. The remaining 19% are international, led by Germany, UK, and France.

Top 15 Nationalities: Benelux Events
🌍
A Local Community with an International Layer

Around 81% of Benelux HYROX athletes are Dutch, Belgian, or Luxembourgish. This is a locally-rooted community event. The remaining 19% are international athletes led by Germany, UK, and France, adding a cross-border layer on top of a strong domestic base.

Athletic Level

What level are they at

The majority of open-division athletes are not elite — they are motivated recreational athletes in the 75 to 115 minute range. That is the largest addressable group in the field.

⏱️
The Achiever Middle

The largest share of open-division athletes finish between 75 and 100 minutes (male) and 85 and 115 minutes (female). This is the motivated recreational majority. Brands and campaigns that speak to improvement and personal achievement resonate most with this group.
