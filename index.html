03 · Participation Loyalty

Who keeps coming back, and what brings them back?

Field sizes have grown 25 times in five seasons, driven largely by first-time participants. Understanding how many athletes return across seasons, and who the loyal core is, points directly to where community-building efforts will have the most impact.

Across all five seasons, around 1 in 10 Benelux athletes returned to race again. This loyal recurring segment is identifiable by name and represents the most engaged part of the community. Two effective strategies to grow this group are performance-based re-engagement, personalised around each athlete's result and natural progression, and social conversion, where existing participants invite friends to race together in doubles or relay formats.

24% → 43%
Team format share of Benelux field (S4 to S8)

Doubles and relay participation has grown from 24% of the Benelux field in Season 4 to 43% in Season 8. Nearly half the field now races with a partner or team. Athletes in team formats carry a shared social commitment to the event, making them more likely to return and more likely to recruit new participants from their network. The growth of team formats is already the most powerful organic retention and community-building engine in the data.

Campaign 01 · Acquisition

From the Sidelines to the Start Line

Target: Audience ticket holders + athletes' networks

The insight

Every HYROX event sells audience tickets. These people are not random bystanders — they came to watch someone they know. They stood at the finish line. They saw the t-shirts, the timing screens, the community. Research on live sports attendance consistently shows that spectators who know a participant personally have the highest conversion rate to first-time participant of any cold audience.

The hook: identity, not discount

Audience ticket holders are not price-sensitive — they already paid to watch. The lever is identity and exclusivity. Not "register now and save 10%" but "you were there at the start. Become part of it."

Mechanic 01

Audience-to-Athlete Badge

Athletes who convert from audience ticket holder to racer receive a special "Sidelines to Start Line" digital badge on their HYROX profile.

Mechanic 02

Early Bird + Priority Wave

Audience ticket holders receive exclusive 48-hour early access to registration for the next event, plus priority start-wave placement.

Campaign 02 · Retention

Your Next PB is One Race Away

Target: Previous Benelux finishers who have not re-registered

The insight

89% of Benelux athletes race once and do not return to a Benelux event. But most of them did not stop being athletes. They are still training, still tracking their fitness, still following HYROX on Instagram. The gap is not motivation — it is activation. Nobody reminded them at the right moment with the right message.

The hook: the PB they know is possible

Every athlete who has finished HYROX knows roughly what they could shave off their time. The campaign makes it personal: "You finished in 1:34:22. Athletes with your profile average 1:24 on their second race."

Mechanic 01

Personalised PB Projection

Email built from the athlete's actual split data. Identifies their slowest station and presents a realistic target time for their second race.

Mechanic 02

Returning Athlete Badge

"Season X Veteran" badge on the race profile for athletes returning to HYROX Benelux. Rewards loyalty with status rather than price.
