06 · Campaign Concepts

Data-informed ideas, ready to activate

Each concept is rooted directly in the data above — not generic sports marketing, but specific opportunities the Benelux audience reveals.

01
"Recruit From The Results"

Every finisher who crosses the line in Amsterdam or Rotterdam is a warm lead for the next event. A retargeting campaign using race-day email data + Instagram Custom Audiences, timed 48–72 hours post-finish when endorphins are still high, converts at 3–5× the rate of cold acquisition.

Data backing: 52k+ finishers across Benelux — a built-in retargeting pool that grows every season.

Channels & Format
Meta retargeting Email automation Strava ads
02
"The 90-Minute Club"

The largest group of open-division athletes finishes between 75–105 minutes — aspirational, not elite. A content series built around this "achiever middle" (training guides, station breakdowns, pacing tips) speaks directly to the majority, not just the podium. Partner with mid-range gear and nutrition brands whose audience matches exactly.

Data backing: Histogram peak at 80–100 min for males, 90–110 min for females.

Channels & Format
YouTube series Instagram Reels TikTok Brand partnership
03
"Benelux Homecoming"

~19% of athletes come from outside Benelux, with Germany, UK, and France as the top feeder markets. A geo-targeted pre-event campaign in those markets — in German, English, and French — captures athletes who specifically travel to race. Even a small uplift in this segment fills hundreds of extra bibs per event.

Data backing: GER, GBR, FRA consistently rank in the top 5 nationalities across all seasons.

Channels & Format
Google Ads (DE/UK/FR) Meta geo-targeting Travel co-marketing

HYROX is a local community event — not yet a destination race

The Amsterdam Marathon draws 65% of its field from abroad, across 130+ nationalities. HYROX Benelux sits at 19% international — confirming it has deep local roots in the Dutch fitness community, but has not yet cracked the same international draw. That gap is both a strength and an opportunity: the domestic foundation is solid, and the ceiling for international growth is clearly high.

Community-first

What this means for strategy

A predominantly local audience calls for a different playbook than a destination race: gym partnerships, Dutch fitness creators, community-building on Instagram and WhatsApp, and local brand co-activations. The international 19% can be grown through geo-targeted campaigns in Germany, UK, and France — the three proven feeder markets in the data.

S4 → S8

Growth is the story

Field sizes grew nearly 25× over five seasons — faster than the Amsterdam Marathon's comparable growth window. HYROX isn't trying to become a marathon. It's building a new category, and the Benelux market is one of its strongest footholds in Europe.

About This Analysis

This dashboard was built as a case study for a digital marketing role application — demonstrating the ability to go from raw data to audience insight to actionable strategy. The analysis combines a Kaggle HYROX dataset (Seasons 4–6, 92k rows) with race results scraped from the HYROX official platform (Seasons 7–8, Benelux only), merged, cleaned, and visualised in a single-page dashboard.

The strategic recommendations are grounded in the Benelux sports market specifically — built on experience working in the Dutch sports industry and understanding how local fitness communities actually behave, not just what the numbers show.
